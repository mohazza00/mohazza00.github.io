Light Is Safe

Light is Safe is a top-down 2D puzzle game created for the Brackeys game jam 2022.1. You play as a little beaver and try to navigate your way in the darkness. The lights keep going out and monsters appear in the darkness and your fear meter increases. You need to find your way to your room by performing the needed tasks to get the key and advance to the next level. You have to use the light switches to turn the lights on to be safe, and see where you're going.
